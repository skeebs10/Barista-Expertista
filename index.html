Hello Coffee Lovers!

This game is designed to test your knowledge on various types of coffee drinks served in coffeeshops worldwide!
Given 3 beans at the start, you the barista, will be asked to pick the correct coffee type for each given recipe.
We're stirring this up! Lose a bean for every incorrect match. You only have 3 beans to complete this
10 drink menu and to be crowned "Barista Expertisa".
